Homemade Gourmet Pizza

There's nothing better than homemade pizza, especially when it's packed with fresh, gourmet ingredients. Here's how you can make your own gourmet pizza at home.

Ingredients:

  • Pizza dough (homemade or store-bought)
  • Fresh mozzarella cheese
  • Tomato sauce
  • Fresh basil, arugula, and other toppings
  • Olive oil, garlic

Directions: Roll out your pizza dough, top with sauce, cheese, and your favorite toppings, and bake in a preheated oven until golden and bubbly. Finish with a drizzle of olive oil and fresh herbs for that gourmet touch.
